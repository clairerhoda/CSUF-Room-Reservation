Welcome to the Study Room Reservation Page
All study room are available in the Pollak Library. Students have a selection of room options depening on their party size or room utlities such as white boards, number of chairs, etc. Rooms can be used from 30 minutes to 3 hours. A room can be reserved no more than 2 weeks in advance. If you would like to reserve a room, click the Reserve a Study Room button below to get started.
