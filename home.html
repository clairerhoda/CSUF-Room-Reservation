Study Room Reservation
Welcome to the Study Room Reservation Page
Important Notices:
-  All study rooms are available in the Pollak Library.
-  Rooms can be used from 30 minutes to 3 hours.
-  A room can be reserved no more than 2 weeks in advance.
-  The latest a room can be reserved is one hour before the starting reservation time.
-  Room sizes vary depending on the amount of people per room.
-  Rooms can be reserved for 1 to 14 people.
-  You can only have one reservation reserved a day.
